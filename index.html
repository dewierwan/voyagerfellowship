The Voyager Fellowship is a three-month residency for exceptional builders working on technologies that protect civilization. The first cohort focuses on biosecurity.

This is an experimental cohort. If you make good progress, you're automatically invited to continue into the full program upon the official launch.

Consider this an invitation to go all in on building humanity's defense.

What You Get

> $25k stipend over three months + materials and travel budget

> Office space at BlueDot London alongside other Voyagers

> High-velocity access to advisors, experts, and follow-on funders

> Strategic red-teaming and field guidance

> Partner/Co-founder matching

Who We're Looking For

Builders who can move fast, test ideas, and pivot when something isn't working. Slope is more important than y-intercept.

The application is short so we reccomend erring on the side of applying.

Process

Application → Interview 1 → Interview 2 → Decision

We aim to decide within two weeks.
