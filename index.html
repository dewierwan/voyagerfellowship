The Voyager Fellowship is a three-month residency for exceptional builders working on technologies that protect civilization. The first cohort focuses on biosecurity.

This is an experimental cohort. If you make good progress, you're automatically invited to continue into the full program.

What You Get

£25k stipend over three months. Office space at BlueDot London alongside other Voyagers. High-velocity access to advisors, technical experts, and funders. Strategic red-teaming and field guidance. Partner matching. Materials and travel budget.

Who We're Looking For

Builders who can move fast, test ideas, and pivot when something isn't working. No PhD required.

Process

Application → Interview 1 → Interview 2 → Decision

We aim to decide within two weeks.
